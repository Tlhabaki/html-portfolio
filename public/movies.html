My three best movies

BEAST OF NO NATION

is a powerful and harrowing film released in 2015. It tells the story of a young boy named Agu, who is forced to become a child soldier in a West African country torn apart by civil war. The film, directed by Cary Joji Fukunaga and based on the novel by Uzodinma Iweala, delves into Agu's emotional and psychological journey as he struggles to survive the brutal realities of war. The movie stars Abraham Attah as Agu and Idris Elba as the ruthless Commandant who leads the group of soldiers. The film received critical acclaim for its intense storytelling and strong performances.


GURDAINS OF THE GALAXY

is a 2014 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. Directed by James Gunn, the movie follows a group of intergalactic misfits who come together to form an unlikely team. The main characters include Peter Quill (also known as Star-Lord), played by Chris Pratt, Gamora (Zoe Saldana), Drax the Destroyer (Dave Bautista), Rocket Raccoon (voiced by Bradley Cooper), and Groot (voiced by Vin Diesel).
